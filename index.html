3) Solar power plant analysis using IoT and solar panel cleaning Machine

Embedded creative design Solution, Nagpur

Domain: Internet of Things (IoT), Power and automation

This project focuses on the instant monitoring of a renewable energy system through IoT based system, for optimizing the efficiency of it. The renewable energy system, that is the solar power plant, is monitored by keeping track of its performance determining parameters that are mainly voltage, current, and power. The voltage parameter from each array of the solar panels is abstracted and converted to a certain lower range, with the help of a voltage sensor, which is sustainable to the microcontroller. Similarly, the current is obtained from the current sensors of each of the solar panels and is further provided to the microcontroller. The monitoring platform is based on the current and voltage measurements of each row of solar panels. The related values are measured with the developed sensing circuits and are further processed by NodeMcu Esp8266 microcontroller. The processed parameters are then stored on the IoT cloud to offer smart data analysis, by the UBIDOTS platform. By the proper interpretation of data over the cloud, the cleansing process can be initiated by the user, by giving commands manually through the blynk application. The cleansing process deals with the dry cleaning mechanism for the system, in which a frame is set onto a particular dusty solar panel of the power system. The brushes made up of soft bristles are used to dust off, the dirt from the panel. Its rotating motion is controlled by the motors.

Further, the frame with this assembly is mounted on the rollers, which helps the frame to traverse from one panel to another, to maintain the efficiency of the solar panels in the power system.

4)Speed Vigilance System

Embedded creative design Solution, Nagpur

Domain: WSN, IoT, Web Service & Communication

Car accidents are a serious issue in our country that needs to be resolved. One of the leading causes of car accidents is overspeeding. The traditional system used signboards for indicating the speed limit of the area. Most of the drivers ignore these signs while driving. This method seemed to be ineffective and has led to an increase in the number of accidents. This project aims to resolve this problem by creating an efficient speed limiting and accident alert system. So to alert the driver about the speed limit at slow speed zones and to detect crashes automatically, it is done by utilizing WSN, GPS, and IoT Technologies. The advantages of the proposed technology are, it saves around 5-6 minutes which are wasted in contacting the hospital and locating the crash site. Secondly, It alerts drivers entering slow speed zones by displaying speed limit, blinking of red LED, and beeping of a buzzer. Finally, the system reduces the speed of electric vehicles within slow-speed zones. This will help in reducing the number of accidents, thereby saving the uncountable number of lives across the country.
